Update of Company Software Portfolio Management System

Consolidation and remediation of outdated software asset portfolio and the automation of future inventory updates

This initiatve came about after an extended period of no maintenance being performed to the Alfabet system for any of the regional application entries by my predesessors. As soon as I assumed the role of Data Steward for the region, I realised that there was some structure and discipline required towards updating and maintaining the Alfabet system. This led to my complete review and rework of the data within my sphere of responsibility.

1. Current state review

- Review the current entries contained within the Alfabet system.

- Check validatity of existing information in Alfabet with the relevant system owners

2. System cleanup

- Remove stale records from Alfabet

- Identify and remove duplicate entries in association with Group Architecture team

3. Engage business stakeholders

- Engage business stakeholders across the region regarding the use of Alfabet system

- Share a summary report of existing info per business are with relevant stakeholders

4. Gather application information

- Request a review from business stakeholders of shared report as well as any additional info available regarding applications in use within their business area

- Consolidate the collected information and compare to existing entries in Alfabet

5. Capture new information

- Capture new entries according to company naming standard

- Extract a new detailed report per business are and provide to relevant stakeholders

- Update Financial info for Systems/Applications

6. Implement system integration & automation

- Review the existing network and application management tool sets available across the organisation

- Implement any extensions required to these discovery tools to facilitate the automation of new software system discovery, including a notification to myself to engage with system owners for additional upon discovery

- Add an additional task to the Change Management template for System Commissioning anddecommissioning to update/notify the Data Steward for the System/Application being commissioned or decommissioned.

7. Maintain Software Catalog info (New system commissioning, old system retirement)

- Review validity of current Alfabet entries

- Check with App Onwrs/Business owners for any lifecycle changes/updates

- Review any Software maintenance agreements up for renewals

8. Management Reporting

- perform quarterly managemernt reports for Lifecycle of applcations

- Compare budget numbers to any applications due for retirement/decommissioning

- Review and report on any savings opportunities w.r.t early retirement of any applications/ systems being used on consumptive basis.
